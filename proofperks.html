Frequently Asked Questions

ProofPerks is a custom-built loyalty program that connects to any Shopify website as a subdomain. It allows both customers and non-purchasers to earn loyalty points that can be redeemed for discount codes and tier-based lifetime perks.

Customers and fans can earn points through various engagement activities:

  • • Commenting on your Instagram posts
  • • Messaging in your Discord community
  • • Leaving Trustpilot reviews
  • • Writing reviews on any review website
  • • Even before making their first purchase

Points can be redeemed for:

  • • Discount codes for immediate savings
  • • Tier upgrades with lifetime discounts
  • • Priority shipping benefits
  • • Exclusive perks based on loyalty level

Users level up to new tiers based on their accumulated loyalty points. Higher tiers unlock lifetime discounts, priority shipping, and exclusive perks that create long-term customer value and repeat engagement.

Strategic Insight

Why Traditional Loyalty Programs Are Killing Your Brand

The $200B loyalty industry has it backwards. Here's why rewarding purchases is the wrong strategy—and what leading brands do instead.

"Every day, another DTC brand launches a 'points for purchases' program. They're all making the same fatal mistake."

Traditional loyalty programs reward the outcome (a purchase) rather than the behaviors that create loyal customers. This creates a transactional relationship where customers only engage when they're ready to buy.

The Hidden Cost of Transaction-Based Loyalty

When you only reward purchases, you're training customers to think of your brand as a vending machine. Insert money, get points. No relationship, no community, no emotional connection.

❌ The Traditional Model

Customer buys → Gets points → Redeems discount → Buys again (maybe)

✅ The Engagement Model

Customer engages → Builds relationship → Earns status → Becomes advocate → Buys repeatedly

This model has three critical flaws:

  1. Late intervention: You're only engaging customers who are already converting
  2. Price sensitivity: You're training customers to wait for discounts
  3. Zero differentiation: Every loyalty program looks the same to customers

The Engagement-First Revolution

Smart brands are flipping the script. Instead of rewarding outcomes, they're rewarding the behaviors that build relationships before customers buy.

What Engagement-First Actually Looks Like

Instead of "spend $100, get 100 points," think:

  • • Leave a detailed product review → Earn points
  • • Share product photos on social → Earn points
  • • Join Discord community conversations → Earn points
  • • Help other customers with questions → Earn points
  • • Attend virtual events or product launches → Earn points

This creates a flywheel: engagement drives social proof, social proof drives more customers, more customers drive more engagement.

The Strategic Advantage

Engagement-first loyalty programs create three competitive advantages:

Community Network Effects

Each engaged customer makes your platform more valuable for every other customer

Authentic Social Proof

Customers create genuine content because they're genuinely engaged, not just paid to post

Data-Rich Customer Profiles

You learn what customers actually care about, not just what they buy

The Implementation Reality

Most brands know engagement-first makes sense but struggle with execution. The technical complexity of tracking cross-platform behaviors, managing point economies, and creating seamless user experiences has kept this strategy limited to enterprises with massive tech teams.

Until now.

The Bottom Line

Traditional loyalty programs optimize for short-term transactions. Engagement-first programs optimize for long-term relationships.

In a world where customer acquisition costs are rising and attention spans are shrinking, the brands that win will be the ones that turn customers into communities.
